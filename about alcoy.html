Alcoy is a charming town situated in the southeastern part of the province of Cebu, Philippines. Blessed with breathtaking natural landscapes, Alcoy offers visitors an unforgettable experience filled with adventure, relaxation, and cultural exploration.

One of the highlights of Alcoy is its stunning coastline, which boasts pristine white-sand beaches and crystal-clear waters. Visitors can spend leisurely days lounging on the sun-kissed shores of Tingko Beach or exploring the vibrant underwater world while snorkeling or diving in the surrounding marine sanctuaries.

In addition to its beautiful beaches, Alcoy is also home to lush green mountains, picturesque waterfalls, and verdant forests waiting to be explored. Hiking enthusiasts can embark on scenic trails leading to hidden waterfalls like Nug-as Falls or take in panoramic views of the town from the summit of Mount Kalandangan.

Alcoy is not only a paradise for nature lovers but also a treasure trove of history and culture. The town's rich heritage is reflected in its colonial-era architecture, historic landmarks, and colorful festivals. Visitors can immerse themselves in the local culture by attending traditional fiestas, sampling delicious Cebuano cuisine, and interacting with friendly locals.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Alcoy offers something for everyone. Come and discover the enchanting beauty and warm hospitality of this hidden gem in the heart of Cebu!
